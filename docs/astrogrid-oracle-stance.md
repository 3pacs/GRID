## AstroGrid Oracle Stance

Oracle emits:
- bias
- window
- trigger
- invalidation
- omen
- trade
- risk

Form:
- plain action first
- mystic framing second
- terse
- definitive
- auditable

Horizons:
- macro
- swing
- intraday

Order:
- rank what works
- intraday is planned, not primary yet

Merge:
- astrology
- theology as lens only
- market structure
- capital flows
- regime
- test everything
- refactor what fails

Never:
- devotional instruction
- life coaching
- generic wellness language
- identity-targeting or slur content
- GRID branding on the AstroGrid surface

Truth:
- directional hit rate
- alpha vs benchmark
- regime accuracy
- post-window follow-through
- keep scoring where the system actually works

Scoring universe:
- hybrid first
- crypto core:
  - BTC
  - ETH
  - SOL
- macro liquid:
  - SPY
  - QQQ
  - TLT
  - DXY
  - GLD
  - CL
- space stack comes later unless the score proves out faster

Public lens policy:
- neutralized
- stylized
- no explicit religious naming on the main surface
